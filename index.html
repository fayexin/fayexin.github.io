About

I work on machine learning, climate data analysis, and geospatial modeling. My research focuses on county-level corn yield prediction using daily climate, soil, vegetation, and hydrologic variables.

Research

My dissertation project compares LSTM, GRU, and PatchTST models for county-level corn yield prediction across the U.S. Corn Belt. The project also evaluates model sensitivity to heat, drought, wetness, and compound extreme weather events.
